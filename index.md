The Earth System Model Evaluation Tool (ESMValTool) is a community developed
software toolkit that aims to facilitate the diagnosis and evaluation of the
causes and effects of model biases and inter-model spread within the CMIP model
ensemble.

> ## What will you learn in this course
>
> - What is ESMValTool / What is the ACCESS ESMValTool-Workflow
> - How to run an ESMValTool recipe on NCI-Gadi using the ESMValTool-Workflow
> - What are the data supported on Gadi?
> - How to develop your own recipes.
> - How to develop your own diagnostics.
> - How to find support.
>
